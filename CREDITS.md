# Credits & Attributions

This project, **ParSec Nova**, is an open-source reimagining of the classic space simulator **ParSec**. We stand on the shoulders of the developers and artists who created the original experience between 1999 and 2002.

## 🏛️ Heritage & Original Project
- **Original Game Concept:** The ParSec Development Team.
- **Source Code Base:** Derived from the GPL-licensed source code maintained by the [OpenParsec Project](https://github.com/OpenParsec).

## 🎨 Asset Attributions (Artwork & Sound)
The visual and auditory identity of ParSec was built by a dedicated community. According to the historical `LICENSE.artwork_sound`, the following individuals contributed key assets:

### 3D Models & Textures
- **Vandarian & K'Sarn Ships:** Original designs and models by the ParSec Art Team.
- **Station Models & Structures:** 
    - Original 3D models and base textures by **Jesper "Zartok" Sjolte**.
    - Additional texture refinements and station variants by **Gunnar "BeS" Benneche** and **Sami "Sekt7" Sjöberg**.
    - Maintenance and conversion for the OpenParsec asset-v2 branch by the **OpenParsec Artwork Team**. 
- **Texture Artists:** Various contributors under CC BY-SA licenses (see historical logs).

### Sound & Music
- **Sound Effects:** Original ParSec sound library contributors (licensed under CC BY-SA / Non-Commercial where noted).
- **Voice Over:** Original "on-board computer" voices from the 1999-2002 era.
